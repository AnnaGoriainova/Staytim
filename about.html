Small businesses are the backbone of every community — run by real people, working hard, building something from nothing. But as AI started transforming industries, most of those tools were built for companies with engineering teams and enterprise budgets.

The technology existed. The access didn't. Customer inquiries went unanswered. Leads went cold. Revenue walked out the door — not because business owners didn't care, but because there was simply no system in place to catch it.

"Small businesses deserve the same technology as the big players. Not a watered-down version of it — the real thing, simple enough to use in three minutes."

So we built Staytim. An AI assistant that any business can set up in minutes — no technical knowledge, no consultants, no enterprise budget required. It answers customer inquiries instantly, follows up with leads automatically, collects reviews, and creates content. Around the clock, without you lifting a finger.

The name comes from the Latin word statim — meaning immediately. Every customer gets a response. Every lead gets followed up. Every time.
