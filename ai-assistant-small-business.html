What is an AI assistant for small businesses?

An AI assistant for small businesses is a tool that handles customer communication automatically — answering questions, taking booking requests, and following up with leads — without you having to be available 24 hours a day.

Unlike large enterprise chatbots that cost thousands of dollars to set up, modern AI assistants like Staytim are designed to be set up in minutes with no technical knowledge, and work across WhatsApp, your website, and Instagram DMs.

The core idea: your customers send a message at any time of day. Instead of waiting hours for a reply — or getting no reply at all — they get an instant, accurate response with your exact pricing, availability, and business information.
